Whisper With Deep Learning Speaker Recognition

In this work, I contributed to advancing assistive communication technologies for the deaf and hard of hearing by integrating Faster Whisper (a real-time ASR model) with a deep learning-based speaker recognition system using ResNet CNN architecture. Through noise augmentation and advanced feature extraction (MFCC with Delta and Double-Delta Coefficients), the model achieved high accuracy and low latency, even in noisy environments. This research opens doors for more robust, real-world applications in assistive communication.
